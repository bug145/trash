About the company and business

We have been engaged in construction, consulting and everything related to the restaurant and hotel business for more than 10 years. Our network has 4 hotels under an international brand and 3 premium restaurants.

Our projects are accompanied by a team of professionals with many years of experience from various business fields, including ethnologists working on author projects.

The trend of growth of domestic tourism and the development of interest in the special traditions of the regions of Central Asia creates a high demand for conceptual hotels with ethnic stuffing and high service in the affordable premium segment.

Pana Boutique Hotels are proven boutique hotels for business and leisure trips.

The main idea is ethnic details combined with modernity to revive the rich traditions of local hospitality.

Pana White Hotel Moscow

A cozy corner in the heart of Moscow's business district, where modern comfort is combined with unique ethno-Kazakh notes. This hotel is an exceptional place where every detail, every night and every morning is transformed into a part of an amazing culture.

A modern hotel near the Baumanskaya metro station, with new generation rooms and breakfast included, is located in the business district of Moscow with excellent transport accessibility, has 128 rooms and conference spaces.

Pana Boutique Hotel (Shymkent)

Pana Boutique Hotel is your gateway to a world where modern hospitality is combined with the rich history and beauty of ethno-Kazakh culture. Our hotel is not just a place to stay overnight, it is a place where every detail is imbued with authenticity, and every moment becomes unforgettable.

The Pana Hotel in Shymkent has 26 rooms, which allows the staff to pay attention to each guest individually.
