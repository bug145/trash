Frequently Asked Questions

You can book by dialing our number or on WhatsApp or write to us by e-mail: panaboutiquehotel1@gmail.com

Is it possible to cancel or adjust the reservation?

It is possible to cancel and change the reservation without penalty on the day of arrival (day), after 50% we charge a fine

At the moment, no, we are considering

What are the check-in and check-out times at your hotels?

Early check-in is possible for early booking from 06.00 am and late check-out until 18.00, the price will be 50% of the room rate.

What forms of payment do you accept?

Cash and non-cash payments (visa, mastercard, QR, Apple pay), payment by bank transfer
